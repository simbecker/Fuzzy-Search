Badminton is a fun sport. Running around, hitting balls, and great fun for up to four people!
And if you happen to be more than four people, then the spare players can rest while four are playing. It is quite
exhausting, so players often want to take a break after a match.
Tan Boon Heong is a professional badminton player and formerly World Number 1 in men's double.
